Scholarships

What is a Scholarship


Scholarships are available for anyone in an under-represented group in tech. This includes (but is not limited to): people of colour, LGBTQIA+ people, people who identify as women and people with disabilities.

We have a limited number of scholarships available which includes a ticket to the ffconf event, travel to Brighton and hotel as required.

How to apply


We will ask a few questions, but don't have to explain your personal situation to us (but you can of course, if you want to). We trust you at this point. We ask who are you? Why you should get the scholarship? Where would you be travelling from?

Please note that your application will be treated with the utmost privacy and that we will not publicly announce any of your details.
