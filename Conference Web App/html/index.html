What?


Future Friends Conference is a full day of eight carefully curated sessions for an audience that cares about the future of the web, and who want their ideas challenged.

This conference was created by Remy and Julie Sharp.

Why?


This annual conference aims to give key speakers a platform to share their knowledge and ideas of web development thus inspiring/teaching others about their field.

It also allows like minded people to connect and share ideas.

Who?


The target audience for this conference is those who have a keen interest in web development. Whether you're a seasoned pro whos just looking for some other peoples take on web development or you are just starting your web development journey and looking to learn this conference is for you.
