Welcome to the Stirling & Mair wesbite

Stirling & Mair are solicitors firm who specialise in commercial legal areas. With a vast amount of experience and expertise which
we use to help our valued customers. Not only are we well versed in law we also like to get to know our customers so we can help
as best as we can.

We specialise in:

  • Conveyancing

  • Powers of Attorney

  • Wills

  • Guardianships

  • Family Law

History of Stirling and Mair

Stirling & Mair was founded in 2002 in Johnstone. In 2004 they moved into their current office building, starting off with a
workforce of just 5 people now Stirling & Mair have over 15 employees. Nowadays we speicalise in a number of fields
however we first began practicing on primarily conveyancing and wills. Located in Johnstone high street Stirling & Mair is the
longest standing solicitors office currently situated in Johnsotne town center.
